Frozen Yogurt Covered Blueberries
100
1 hour
familyfreshmeals.com
>6 oz fresh blueberries
>6 oz nonfat greek yogurt
>baking sheet
done
>Start by washing your blueberries and lining a small baking sheet with parchment or wax paper.
>Using a toothpick, dip each blueberry into the Greek yogurt and swirl until the blueberry is nicely coated with yogurt. Place on a baking sheet. Continue this until all blueberries are coated.
>Place the baking sheet into the freezer, and let freeze for at least an hour.
>After about an hour, your Frozen Yogurt Covered Blueberries can be placed in a ziplock bag and stored in the freezer. Take out what you need for snack time and enjoy!
