Chilled Overnight Chia 
365
Overnight
goodhousekeeping.com
>½ cup of old fashioned oats
>1 tbsp chia seeds
>1 tbsp honey
>milk / unsweetened plant-based milk
>16 oz container with lid
>peanut butter + jam
>fruit of choice
done
>Add oats, chia seeds and honey to the container.
>Fill the remaining of the container with milk of choice until ⅔ full.
>Close the container and shake to combine. Refrigerate overnight.
>When ready to serve, peanut butter + jam and/or fruit of choice may be added for additional flavoring.
